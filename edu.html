Education

In this next graph there is a comparison between ethnicity ratio among Ivy League students. These universities have a considerable amount of international students so we can generally expect a small decrease on the ratios for white and black ethnicities and an increase on others.

Generally both ethnicities have a reduction on their ratios. Taking into account the difference on the population, the reduction of the ratio on white and black ethnicities are very similar. This might seem to contradict the idea that the black community has less opportunities in education, but that is not entirely true. It's not contradicting, but instead showing that the Ivy League colleges are doing a pretty good job at having diversity among the students. It might not be the case in other levels of education and other groups of colleges.
